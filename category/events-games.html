8DAYS finalist for the AzPlay International Festival of Independent Games

[caption id="attachment_1319" align="aligncenter" width="550"] Lola and Mike celebrating the nomination![/caption]

We are very proud to announce that ‪8DAYS‬ has been nominated to the AzPlay International Festival of Independent Games.

This is our second game in a row that gets nominated as Best Basque Videogame Award. Last year ‪HassleHeart‬ made it and won the award, we hope that 8DAYS gets the chance to make it too.

Thank you all for your support!

HassleHeart Best Basque Videogame at hóPLAY'14!

[caption id="attachment_1270" align="aligncenter" width="700"] Definition of happiness[/caption]

Yes, HassleHeart won the BEST BASQUE VIDEOGAME AWARD at the fifth International Festival of Independent Games hóPLAY !!

[caption id="attachment_1263" align="aligncenter" width="700"] @ipuntom and @gravstar trying to make a congrats speach... Photo by: Eva Zubero Zuluaga[/caption]

The Festival started very well, as nominated, we had the oportunity to showcase HassleHeart and have a panel to talk about the game and our studio.

[caption id="attachment_1274" align="aligncenter" width="700"] @gravstar during the panel. First HassleHeart sketches were unveiled ;)[/caption]

We had a great time talking with other studios, media and people who came to the hóPLAY Festival to try and play games (HassleHeart included!).

[caption id="attachment_1268" align="aligncenter" width="700"] Networking area[/caption]

[caption id="attachment_1273" align="aligncenter" width="700"] @gravstar trying the Fronton Occulus Rift version from our @PulsarConcept friends[/caption]

[caption id="attachment_1272" align="aligncenter" width="700"] People playin (and enjoying!!) HassleHeart[/caption]

As hóPLAY finalist developer, Santa Clara Games had the chance to explain HassleHeart in the Big Indie Pitch to Steel Media journalists. What an intense and very funny experience!

[caption id="attachment_1269" align="aligncenter" width="700"] We came to play so, get ready to pitch![/caption]

We were so happy and so surprised words couldn't get out of our mouths! It's been a wonderful moment! It gives us so much energy and fuel to keep making games that we are looking forward to December 23^rd^ to launch HassleHeart's Final Version and start working in our next games! Yes, games in plural ;) Stay tunned for more news to come!

[caption id="attachment_1267" align="aligncenter" width="700"] Alea jacta est[/caption]

We want to thank you all, without your support this wouldn't have come true, THANK YOU SO MUCH!! \<3 \<3 \<3

Santa Clara Games will be at the Global Game Jam 2014 in Donostia

The Global Game Jam 2014 is coming to our city, and Santa Clara Games will not only be taking part, but sponsoring it as well!

We've been pushing to bring the Global Jam 2014 to Donostia. Santa Clara Games will be organizing the event along with the folks from Pintxo Developer.

Mr. Pixelsmith (@ipuntom) and the Undead Pirate Captain (@gravstar) will both be getting down and dirty. The Heart-Core developer @twikoon is on a trip to the USA, but he shoud be with us via Google Hangouts. We will be tweeting and uploading photos and videos of the event as well as our super-amazing, astonishngly incredible, made-in-48-hours game.

Stay tuned!
