HassleHeart is an amazing arcade game about a robot running out of energy forced to rip out hearts to survive.

All along the levels in which the videogame is divided, Roboto will discover the existence of the legendary Golden Heart which gives immortality to whom will find it. To get it, he will face a lot of funny characters. 		 

HassleHeart is a very addictive arcade game with simple mechanics.

The player will be able to control Roboto with WASD and SPACE keys, ripping out hearts from people on the street. But the task will not be as simple as it seems, because people will escape when Roboto approaches.

In order to chase them, Roboto will find bait inside mysterious boxes. Once he has set the bait on the floor he only has to move away and wait until someone gets trapped. That's the moment to rip out the victim's heart and get their energy.

Explore five different levels and discover the myth of the Golden Heart, which gives immortality to whom gets it from the "Final Boss".

Be careful, energy is scarce and people will fight for their lifes!

Roboto will face unique and funny characters through the city.

Each level has its own and unique characters: Junkies and Policemen at the Indie Ghetto, Soccer moms and Hipsters at Suburbia, Fatso and Bussinessmen at Downtown... and more to discover! Unveil them all by unlocking all the levels.

Each level has its own and special chiptune soul.
